## Variables

**What do all variables have?**

> Variables consist of a **name**, **type**, and **value**.

**Variables have to be declared before use.**

> **True** Variables must be declared before than can be used.

**The same variable may be declared twice**

> The same variable **cannot** be declared twice.

**What are some examples of C# legal and illegal variable names?**

> **Legal**: score, number3, student_grade, ZELDA

> **Illegal**: 7Gnomes, $file
